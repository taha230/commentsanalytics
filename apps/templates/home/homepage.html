Sentiment Analysis

Sentiment analysis is contextual mining of text that extracts subjective information in the source materials and helps businesses to understand the social sentiment of their brand, product or content while monitoring online comments.

Sentiment analysis looks at the emotion encoded in a text. It is widly used to analyze customer feedback, survey responses, and video comments. Social media monitoring, reputation management, and customer experience are just a few areas that can benefit from sentiment analysis.

Key Phrases Extraction

Keyword phrase extraction is commonly used to extract key information from a series of comments. It is a text analysis method that involves Natual Language Processing (NLP) methods to extract the most important words and expressions from the text.

You’ll be able to see what topics are causing the most discussion among your consumers in comments, and automating the process will save your customer service unit time. Word clouds or tag clouds are another examples of keyword extraction. They show visualizations of a text’s most frequently used words in word clusters.
