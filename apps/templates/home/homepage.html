Sentiment Analysis

Sentiment analysis is contextual mining of text that extracts subjective information in the source materials and helps businesses to understand the social sentiment of their brand, product or content while monitoring online comments.

Sentiment analysis looks at the emotion encoded in a text. It is widly used to analyze customer feedback, survey responses, and video comments. Social media monitoring, reputation management, and customer experience are just a few areas that can benefit from sentiment analysis.
