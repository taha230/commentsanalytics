The role of Youtube as new social network?

Recently, Youtube is the most well-known video-based social network for people and companies to search for their interests by watching videos.With more than 2.5 billion monthly active users, it has become an important part of many people's online experience. The platform is considered mainly a video-sharing website where users can watch and upload videos. However, its other features like subscription, short stories, comments, and polls also provide interaction between users.

It can be argued that YouTube does not satisfy the definition of a social media platform because it doesn't have instant messaging capabilities like other social media platforms such as Facebook and Twitter. But it can also be argued that YouTube qualifies as a social media platform because it provides some degree of interactivity with other users via its community posts, comments, likes, and shares.

Paying a proportion of advertising income to Youtuber, make Youtube boost in recent five years. There are many video content provider that works as Youtuber and earns money by monetizing their channels by view count from their audience. Showing relevant advertising during watching videos according to the interest and history of users is the key point for marketing on Youtube. With this strategy, both companies and Youtuber can reach their goals. Actually, this winning strategy encourages people to create their own video content and attract their subscribers to be a partner of youtube in earning advertising income. Consequently, more people are willing to watch movies including product reviews, film suggestions, music reactions, artwork tutorials, learning courses, or even daily vlogs on Youtube.
