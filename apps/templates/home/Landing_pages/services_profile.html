Type of profile

In profile browse profile finder services, we classify the profile platforms into four main categories including Business profiles, People profiles, General-Entity profiles, and blogging platforms. All profile-driven platforms are categorized into these four main classes.

  • Business profile category

The first category includes websites that have the capability for companies to import their public data and attract their followers. Websites such as Zoominfo, Crunchbase, and Amazon business pages categorized in this Business profile category.

  • People profile category

People profile category mainly focused on people having their own profiles to share their experiences, interests, and activities. These platforms help people to construct a Comprehensive database on specific subjects. For example, a database of research papers on Google scholar or a database of code repositories on Github. These people data are mainly used for recruiting the people and finding the best match.

  • Blogging profile category

The third category includes a website that has the blogging features for publishing articles, news, and comments on subjects. Reddit, Livejournal, and Tumblr are classified in this category.

  • General Entities profile category

The last profile data category is General profiles that assign to the platforms that are not developed for People, Blogging and Businesses. A platform such as Wikipedia for general information, Booking for trip database, or Imdb for film data platform.
